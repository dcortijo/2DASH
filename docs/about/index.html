Controls

Use the cursor keys to move left and right and to jump. You won't get your maximum speed right away, instead you will accelerate slowly and you have to try to mantain your speed.

Press Z and X to swing your whip at your enemies and stop them from attacking you back. Also, if you are in the air, you'll stall your fall momentarily.
